Fitness Connection Mobile App - Features
========================================

**1\. App Positioning and Core Direction**

**Fitness Connection App Positioning**

The app should be positioned as a fitness connection app rather than a plain dating app. Thisgives it a stronger identity and also supports better App Store approval chances, because duringthe discussion it was clearly noted that launching it purely as a dating app may create reviewrisk on iOS.

**Fitness First, Not Social Media First**

The app should not feel like a content-posting or influencer-style platform. It should focus onintentional connection, discipline, consistency, authenticity, and fitness lifestyle, which is alsoconsistent with the client saying he did not want the product to become a place where usersare only trying to be seen.

**User Login**
